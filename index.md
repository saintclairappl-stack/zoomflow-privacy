# ZoomFlow Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

ZoomFlow does not collect, store, or share your personal data.

## Permissions Used
- **Camera**: To capture photos and videos. Never transmitted off your device.
- **Photo Library**: To save and display your captured media on your device only.
- **Microphone**: To record audio during video capture. Saved locally only.

## Local Storage
App settings are stored locally on your device only.

## In-App Purchases
Handled by Apple App Store. Purchase verification uses RevenueCat with anonymous IDs only.
